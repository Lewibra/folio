HowTo Run:
You will need codeblocks to compile this project,
open RUN.cpb in the objReader folder and set it to Debug next to the Run/Compile
button.
Click run and compile



CONTROLS:

A -- move left
D -- move right
S -- speed up
Z -- Drop to the floor
X -- spiral to the floor
R -- reset position
G -- Turn everything green!
L -- Disperse
O -- Re Disperse
Down -- Zoom out
Foward -- zoom in
Left, right -- rotate
