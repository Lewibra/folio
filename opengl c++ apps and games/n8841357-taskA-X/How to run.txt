HowTo Run:
You will need codeblocks to compile this project,
open Run.cpb in objReader and set it to Debug next to the Run/Compile
button.
Click run and compile



Controls 

f1 - rotate the display
up - lighting off
down - lighting on
left - expand
right - contract
L click - speed up
L click on cubes to move them (click once and it follows the mouse, click again to make it stop)
R click - slow down
